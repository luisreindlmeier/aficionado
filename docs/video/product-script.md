# Product video, 60 seconds

Goal: make a judge from the Maschmeyer Group feel, in one minute, that we turned their own
founder thesis into a working machine, and prove it by letting the machine judge the presenter
live and honestly. Tone: calm, confident, a little cool. Let the product and the receipts do
the talking. Voiceover is Luis, first person, because the demo evaluates Luis.

## Before you record

- **The opening clip.** Carsten Maschmeyer, brutkasten interview, 22.10.2024.
  Source video: `https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EIZRV91Q1-M`. The line lands **early**, it is
  his answer to the **first question**, roughly the first 30 to 90 seconds. **Scrub to confirm
  the exact second** and clip the 4 to 6 seconds where he says "Fuer mich sind immer die Personen
  wichtiger als das Produkt." Keep his real audio under this beat. If clip licensing is a concern,
  fall back to the quote on a clean title card with the citation, spoken in VO.
- **Screen capture.** Record the app at retina resolution so Space Grotesk and the thin borders
  stay crisp. Capture the Radar populating, the Luis dossier streaming in, a real receipt click
  through to the `transformer-lob` GitHub page, and the verdict card.
- **Numbers are real.** Every figure below is a live computed score. Do not restyle them.

## Shot list

1. **Clip, 0 to 6s.** Maschmeyer quote, real audio, citation lower-third. This is the emotional anchor, do not rush off it.
2. **Wordmark cut, 6 to 9s.** Clean white frame, wordmark snaps in. This is the hard cut, let the line land.
3. **Radar auto-populate, 9 to 16s.** Screen capture of Loop A filling the Radar. Speed-ramp to ~1.5x so rows arrive with energy.
4. **Discover and open Luis, 16 to 23s.** Cursor selects the Luis row, dossier streams in as agent traces.
5. **Proof receipt, 23 to 33s.** Rings animate, expand the transformer-lob receipt, click through to the live repo so the judge sees the real source.
6. **Gravity honesty, 33 to 43s.** Hold on the greyed, excluded Gravity and the "add LinkedIn" flag. This beat is the whole pitch, give it room.
7. **Verdict, 43 to 52s.** The 62 / 100 Watch card assembling with the percentile line.
8. **Sources push, 52 to 57s.** Slow move across the dossier showing every number is a link.
9. **Tagline, 57 to 60s.** Wordmark and "people over product, made mechanical" on white.

## Notes

- Keep the palette pure. Near-white background, near-black text, the only colour is the tiny metric
  and status dots (Proof teal, Gravity violet, Trajectory blue, Watch amber).
- No music swell over the Maschmeyer clip, let his voice carry it. Bring in a minimal,
  low pulse after the hard cut at 6 seconds.
- Total voiceover is roughly 80 words, comfortable for 54 seconds of speaking with pauses.
- The single most important frame is the honest Gravity exclusion at 33 to 43 seconds. If a viewer
  remembers one thing, it should be that the machine admitted what it did not know.
